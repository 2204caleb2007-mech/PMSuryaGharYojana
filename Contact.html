Frequently Asked Questions

What is the PM Surya Ghar Yojana scheme?

PM Surya Ghar Yojana is a government initiative to promote rooftop solar installation in residential areas by providing subsidies and financial assistance.

How can I check if my rooftop is suitable for solar installation?

Use our AI-powered verification tool to analyze your rooftop and check eligibility for subsidies under the scheme.

What documents are required for subsidy application?

You'll need proof of identity, address proof, property ownership documents, and electricity bill copies. Our team can guide you through the process.

How long does the verification process take?

Our AI analysis typically takes 2-3 minutes. The complete subsidy approval process may take 2-4 weeks depending on your location and documentation.
