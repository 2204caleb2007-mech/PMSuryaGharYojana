Application Process

Follow these simple steps to apply for the PM Surya Ghar Yojana and start your journey towards free solar electricity.

01

Visit Website or Download App

Visit our official website or download our mobile application to get started with your application.

02

Navigate to Consumer Page & Click "Apply Now"

Go to the consumer page and select "Apply Now" to begin your application process.

03

Enter Mobile Number & Verify

Enter your valid registered consumer mobile number, complete the captcha verification, and agree to the guidelines by selecting "Yes, I have read all the guidelines of PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana." Then click "Verify".

04

Enter OTP & Login

Enter the valid mobile OTP received through SMS and click on "Login" to proceed with your application.

05

Complete Profile Details

After successful login, enter your profile details including consumer name, email, address, state, district, and PIN code. Click "Save" to continue.

06

Submit Solar Rooftop Application

Submit your application either by clicking "Apply for Solar Rooftop" or through vendor selection. Select your state, district, and electricity distribution company/utility. Enter your consumer account number and click "Fetch Details". After loading your details, click "Next" to complete your application submission.

Important Notes

Document Requirements

Make sure to have your electricity bill, identity proof, and address proof ready before starting the application process.

Processing Time

Applications are typically processed within 15-30 days after submission of all required documents.

Need Help?

Contact our helpline at 1800-180-3333 or email support@suryaghar.gov.in for assistance with your application.

Eligibility Check

Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria before applying to avoid delays in processing your application.
